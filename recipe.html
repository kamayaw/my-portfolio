Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A simple and easy grilled cheese sandwich recipe.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Any type of cheese
  • A pan

Steps

  1. Heat a pan on medium heat
  2. Add butter on the pan and let it melt.
  3. Place one slice of bread onto the buttered pan.
  4. Place cheese on top of the bread.
  5. Add the second slice of bread on top of the cheese.
  6. Cook until the bread is golden brown and then carefully flip it over.
  7. Cook the other side until the cheese melts and the bread is evenly toasted.
